About Us

Who We Are

Book Bhandar is an online platform that brings together books from trusted local bookshops. We work directly with neighborhood bookshops to offer a wide range of new releases, novels, and hard-to-find titles, all in one convenient place.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make books easily available to you while supporting Islamabad's local bookstores. We aim to keep the city's reading culture active by combining the ease of online shopping with the reliability of local sourcing.
